Whirligig Blade Designer

A whirligig's performance is governed by the balance between Starting Torque and Momentum.

Balanced (38%): Best all-around performance.

Snappy: Low inertia, starts spinning in a whisper of wind.

High Coast: High inertia, acts as a flywheel to spin through wind lulls.
